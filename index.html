My name is Halley Caldwell

Three months before graduating, with a Bachelors in Political Science, I realized I had chosen the wrong career path. Throughout my college days I found myself looking into the Computer Science program. However, the program seemed daunting so I stayed on the Politcal Science track.


After college I reflected heavily on where my passions really were. I began to self teach myself HTML, CSS, and small bit of Javascript. I was blown away by what a few lines of code were capable of! I knew I wanted to take this to the next level, something that was past self-teaching and offered a more structured learning environment. I wasn't ready for another fully fledged college expereince so I began to search for bootcamps in my local area.


I applied to the Southern Methodist University coding bootcamp and was accepted. Being in the course made me realize two things. 1) I was right, programs related to Computer Science are daunting. 2) I liked the challenge. I LOVED the challenge and still do.


This field is always changing, always evolving, and I could not be more ecstatic about that. I am finally pursuing a career that aligns with what I'm passionate about.
